# Legal and Content Policy

CouchTV is intended to aggregate and present legal free-to-air/publicly accessible streams only.

## Core Policy

- Do not include links that require paid subscriptions or unauthorized bypass.
- Do not include streams known to violate copyright.
- Respect stream source terms and takedown requests.
- Remove reported links quickly if ownership/licensing is disputed.

## Operational Rules

- Source metadata from public IPTV-org listings.
- Apply filtering rules to reduce unsafe/inappropriate content.
- Keep auditability: generated feed artifacts should be reproducible from pipeline inputs.

## Public Release Notes

- Include a clear disclaimer that channel rights belong to original broadcasters.
- State that stream availability and legality can vary by jurisdiction.
- Provide issue reporting channel for incorrect listings or removals.
